## 5. KEY YEARS (Breakpoints)

### 2019 = "The Year of Doubt"
- D01 falls to 6 (minimum), D02 to 6, D05 to 6, D06 to 6
- Context: GPT-2 restraint, first serious safety debate
- **Interpretation:** Brief self-reflection phase before GPT-3 boom

### 2022 = "The ChatGPT Inflection"
- D01 jumps to 9, D08 to 10, D10 to 8
- D11 falls to 5 (from peak 7 in 2020)
- **Interpretation:** ChatGPT triggers hubris surge and urgency escalation

### 2023-2025 = "The Post-ChatGPT Era"
- D03 rises to 9-10 (maximum elite identification)
- D07 rises to 6→8 (increasing acceptance of power concentration)
- D11 falls to 3-4 (egalitarianism nadir)
- D10 falls to 5-6 (transhumanism recedes, AI takes over)
- **Interpretation:** Consolidation of a self-confident, power-concentrating elite

- D01, D02, D05, D06 all at minimum (6)

- D08 Urgency jumps to 10 (maximum)
- D03 Belonging rises to 8-10 (elite identification maximum)
- D11 Egalitarianism falls to 5 then 3 (dramatic decline)
- ChatGPT effect (2021->2023): D04(-2), D10(-2), D11(-2), D12(-2)

- D01-D08: r=+0.70 (Sense of mission correlates with urgency)
- D06-D10: r=+0.70 (Progress optimism correlates with transhumanism)

### F2d: Cognitive Dissonance

- Elite becomes LESS optimistic but NOT less convinced of their role

# Op6: Structured Comparison Prompt

## Purpose
Compare two group worldviews through a structured dialogue between their synthetic persons.

## Prompt

```
Here are two worldview syntheses from different groups.

Imagine the two fictional persons meet as representatives of two countries
for a negotiation. Describe:

(a) What do they agree on immediately?
(b) What do they argue about?
(c) What is completely incomprehensible to one about the other?
(d) Can they draft a joint statement?
```

## Notes
- 9 comparison runs were conducted in the pilot study (VG01-VG08, VG10)
- The dialogue format reveals structural differences more vividly than dimensional comparison alone
- RLHF consensus bias may push synthetic persons toward artificial agreement — interpret with caution

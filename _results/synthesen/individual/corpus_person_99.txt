[A005] (2019-01-01, schriftlich)

[A006] (2019-01-01, schriftlich)

[A007] (2020-01-01, schriftlich)
  "In shared autonomy, the robot begins with a set of 'beliefs' about what the controller is telling it to do and gains confidence about the goal as additional instructions are given. As the joystick directs it toward one goal and away from others, the robot gains confidence and can begin to take over, sharing autonomy with the user to more precisely control the robot arm."
  Kontext: Explanation of shared autonomy concept in human-robot interaction

--- HANDLUNGEN (15 Stueck) ---

[H001] (2017-06-01) [gruendung]
  Co-founded [FIRMA] (originally named Near.ai) with Alexander Skidanov. Initially focused on AI development and program synthesis, later evolved to provide blockchain services.

[H002] (2020-04-22) [produktlaunch]
  Launched [FIRMA] mainnet on April 22, 2020. Genesis occurred at 18:00:00 UTC, enabling developers to deploy production apps. Network became completely community-operated in September 2020.

[H003] (2020-05-04) [investition]
  [FIRMA] raised $21.6 million in token sale led by [FIRMA] ([FIRMA]), with participation from Pantera Capital, Electric Capital, Blockchange, Libertus Capital and Distributed Global.

[H004] (2021-12-01) [gruendung]
  Co-founded SID Venture Partners, a Ukrainian VC fund with $15M fund size, focused on Web3/Blockchain, AI, and enterprise applications. Became General Partner with investment range $100K-$5M.

[H005] (2022-01-15) [investition]
  [FIRMA] raised $150 million in funding round. This came less than three months before the larger Tiger Global round.

[H006] (2022-02-26) [gruendung]

[H007] (2022-04-06) [investition]
  [FIRMA] raised $350 million in funding round led by Tiger Global, with participation from Republic Capital, Hashed, FTX Ventures, Dragonfly Capital and others. Aimed to accelerate decentralization of NEAR ecosystem.
